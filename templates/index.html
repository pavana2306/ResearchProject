Applying Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers (BERT) and Vader for Sentiment Analysis on Pharmaceutical Drug Reviews

Main objective of this research is to perform sentiment analysis on the Pharma Drug Reviews using Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers (BERT) and combine the results of the sentiment score from Vader Sentiment to obtain the overall sentiment score.
